Interest in IT

My interest in IT has always been there as a curiosity but one I never fully explored. Lately, my interest was truly sparked with my exploration of computational biology as a potential career path. The field of biological sciences is trending in the direction of increasing reliance of computers and data science, with more and more lab techniques providing massive datasets requiring in depth computer-aided analysis. As a result, I have begun to delve into the world of programming and IT, which has brought me to this course.

Why I Chose RMIT?

Around 7 years ago I was contemplating whether to move to Melbourne to go to RMIT or stay with my parents and go to UNSW in Sydney but did not end up going through with either plan. Fast forward to 2020, living in Melbourne, in the midst of Covid-19 lockdowns with nothing to do, I googled online science degrees and one of the first to pop up was Bachelor of Science at RMIT, I took that as a good omen and applied the next day.
