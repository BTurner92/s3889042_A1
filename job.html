Role Description and Appeal

What makes this role most appealing is the company, ever since I found out about CSL I have wanted to figure out how I could be a part of what they do. They are a biotechnology company specialising in researching and designing vaccines, drugs, antivenoms, and other lifesaving technologies. The role advertised is that of a senior data engineer, responsible for structuring and maintaining data storage systems, enabling easy access and analysis by data scientists and bioinformaticians. With other key roles being in support of the data scientists and researchers to troubleshoot errors and improve data modelling capabilities.

Description of Required Skills

  • A Bachelor’s or master’s in computer science, data science, software engineering, bioinformatics or related fields.
  • Advanced programming knowledge and skills (5+ years) utilising languages such as python, R, SAS, SQL, and other database query languages.
  • Advanced knowledge of Data science and visualisation concepts (tools and applications).
  • Experience with data management principles (FAIR), standards, methods, and platforms.
  • Experience in handling both structured and unstructured data from a variety of sources (real world, lab, clinical etc.)
  • A familiarity with Amazon Web Service or other cloud platforms.
